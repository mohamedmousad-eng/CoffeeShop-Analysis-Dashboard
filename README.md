# CoffeeShop-Analysis-Dashboard
# Retail Sales & Transaction Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on operational optimization for a multi-location coffee retail business. By analyzing raw transactional data, I built a performance tracker to solve real-world challenges related to staffing efficiency, inventory management, and revenue growth.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Tool:** Microsoft Excel
* **Functions:** Pivot Tables, VLOOKUP/INDEX-MATCH, Advanced Charting, Data Cleaning
* **Analysis Focus:** Temporal Trends, Geographic Performance, Operational Optimization

## ❓ Problem Statement
The business faced several operational hurdles:
* **Inefficient Staffing:** Difficulty predicting "rush hours," leading to either overstaffing or poor customer service.
* **Inventory Mismanagement:** No clear data on which products to prioritize during high-growth periods.
* **Geographic Variance:** A lack of comparative data between the **Lower Manhattan** and **Astoria** branches.

## 💡 Solution: The Performance Tracker
I developed a dynamic Excel-based tracker that visualizes:
1. **Monthly Revenue Trends:** Tracking the trajectory of business growth.
2. **Hourly Transaction Volumes:** Pinpointing exactly when customers are most active.
3. **Branch-Specific Metrics:** Comparative analysis for specific urban locations.

## 📊 Key Results & Impact
* **Revenue Surge:** Identified a **150% revenue increase** from January to June, allowing for proactive inventory scaling.
* **Peak Hour Identification:** Discovered that **10:00 AM** is the peak transaction hour across branches.
* **Data-Driven Scheduling:** Shifted staffing models to align with peak demand, reducing wait times and optimizing labor costs.

## 🏁 Conclusion
By transforming thousands of rows of transaction data into a centralized visual dashboard, this project enabled the business to move from "best-guess" management to **evidence-based decision making**.
